About Naloxone Advocates Plymouth CIC

We provide free naloxone training, overdose response education, and practical harm‑reduction support across Plymouth and Devon.

Our Mission

To reduce overdose deaths by empowering people with knowledge, naloxone, and non‑judgemental community action.

Training

Short, practical sessions for community members, peers, and organisations.

Access

Connecting people with naloxone kits and harm‑reduction resources.

Advocacy

Working with partners to improve local responses and reduce stigma.

How We Work

  • Evidence‑based, trauma‑informed, and person‑centred.
  • Peer‑to‑peer models to reach people where they are.
  • Partnerships with services, shelters, and community groups.

Team & Partners

We’re a community‑interest company supported by volunteers, peer trainers, and partner organisations across Devon.
